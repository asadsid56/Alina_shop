About us

Our suits are ...

Traditional salwar kameez made in a fabric known as ‘lawn’. Originally made from linen, Lawn is now made from cotton and is one of the lightest fabrics that are part of an Indian design house’s collection of textiles. Ideal for warm weather, Lawn suits are all about flaunting prints, designs, colors and everything else that is vibrant on a bright summer day.
